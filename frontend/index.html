Gotham (2014)

Before Batman, there was Gotham. A new recruit to the Gotham City Police Department, James Gordon, pairs up with veteran detective Harvey Bullock to solve one of Gotham's most notorious cases: the murder of Thomas and Martha Wayne. During his investigation, Gordon meets Bruce, the Wayne's son, now in the care of his butler Alfred Pennyworth. The encounter with the young Wayne further impels Gordon to catch the mysterious killer. Along the way, Gordon becomes involved with Gotham's mob families and associates, including gangster Fish Mooney. Eventually, Gordon is forced to befriend Wayne, a friendship that will help shape the boy's future to become the Dark Knight.
